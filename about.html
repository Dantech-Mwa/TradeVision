About TradeVision

TradeVision is a free, open-source, and fully static trading platform built for traders who want TradingView-level features without the cost or complexity.

100% client-side. No backend. No login required. Works offline. Host on GitHub Pages in 60 seconds.

Built with Lightweight Charts, LocalStorage, and pure HTML/CSS/JS.
